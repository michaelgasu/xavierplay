Technology Transfer ← Return to services

Technology transfer is a subset of IP licensing and is the process of commercially exploiting research output often requires having to transfer knowledge, technologies, manufacturing techniques and their attendant IP rights from R&D facilities to corporate vehicles geared toward production based on this know-how. This is usually done via licensing agreements, outright assignments, or even joint ventures, partnerships or spin-offs, usually depending on venture capital funding requirements or business development strategy.

Whether the transfer is vertical (from research units to production units) or horizontal (between applied research units and research and development units), we can assist in building the legal frameworks that will facilitate the technology transfer process.
